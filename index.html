My name is Natalie and Especially Made is my pride and joy! My successful and thriving little business has been providing beautiful stationery for a whole range of events for almost 10 years with handmade wedding stationery being our speciality. We pride ourselves on the personal service we offer where you work with us to get exactly what you want. There are no set ‘templates’ that we work to, so if you want something just ask and we will find a way to achieve it with added pizzazz!

We are aware that for some couples money is no object whilst for others a set budget for their wedding stationery is very important, so let us create something beautiful for you that fits your exact requirements.

We are excited that you are considering working with us, so call  us and be inspired!

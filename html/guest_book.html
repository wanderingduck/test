The image above shows a cream guest book with box, both decorated with dusky pink ribbon and Swarovski crystal details to match the couple’s wedding stationery design.

Utterly luxurious boxed guest books!

It took us a long time to find the perfect guest book, and we think this is it!! The books  are exquisitely well made, and exude quality!

The books have 40 lovely thick pages which give your guests 80 sides on which to write wedding messages!  Each guest book measures a generous 200mm x 250mm. The box measures approximately 250mm x 300mm. Each book also comes with a 100 cm piece of cream or ivory ribbon to match the book, great if the book is a gift.

The books come in a gorgeous rich cream (main image) or an ivory / white, and both come with fully matching boxes. (see images of plain books to the right. The books also have a matching ribbon page marker.

Decoration -Simply choose a ribbon colour to match your wedding theme and we will decorate the box and the guest book to match. You can choose whether you would like a loose bow or flat bow  to decorate the book and box. The different bow options are shown below.

Embellish – the book can be decorated with Swarovski crystals too. The cover of the book can be scattered with little sparkly diamonds for a bit of glamour. If you prefer then you can choose an embellishment instead. See your embellishment options below.

Little 3mm Swarovski crystals for scattering on the cover of the guest book. They sparkle beautifully. We can  also place one in the centre of the bow as shown in the images below.

Decoration

As shown above, your book will be decorated with the bow of your choice, and either a scattering of Swarovski crystals, OR one of the embellishments shown below. Please let us know which you would prefer.

1. Heart ribbon buckle.
This will be placed in the centre of the ribbon on the front of the book.

2. Square ribbon buckle.
This will be placed at the centre of the ribbon on the front of the book.

3. Full square.
This will be placed at the centre of the ribbon at the front of the book

4. Feather butterfly.
This will be placed at the centre of the bow on the front of the book. In cream or white to match the book colour.

5. Swarovski Crystal heart.
This will be sewn into the centre of the bow on the front of the book.

Ribbon colours - We use only the highest quality ribbons for our stationery.

  • Luxurious double faced satins that tie beautifully and look utterly sumptuous
  • Wonderfully light, sheer organza ribbons for a more delicate look. These ribbons have a subtle light reflecting quality.

Both ribbons are available in a huge range of gorgeous colours. If you do not see your colour, simply email us and we will see what we can do to help.

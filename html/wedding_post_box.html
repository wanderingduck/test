STANDARD POST BOX DETAILS

Size and finishes
Post boxes measure 50cm tall x 25cm wide and deep.
They are constructed from rigid board and have a lift off lid.
The slot in the lid is wide enough for even the biggest of wedding cards.
Boxes are available in an ivory or kraft finish. Bespoke colours are available at an additional charge which will be confirmed on placing your order.

Decoration
Our standard post boxes are actually anything but standard.
The lid is wrapped in a band of ribbon in the colour of your choice.
This is finished with a bow and embellishment. These are shown below.
The front of the box has a wording panel and this panel also features the same bow and embellishment.

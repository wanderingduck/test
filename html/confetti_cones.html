Base and cones  

Our confetti cone sets include an ivory base box and 25 gorgeous parchment paper cones. The box and cones are ready assembled so there is no fiddly sticking to do. The base box (shown right) has pre-cut holes, each of which supports an individual confetti cone. The base itself is incredibly strong and has a lovely linen texture.

Confetti

You can either buy the cone set and fill it with your own confetti, or you can purchase a set from us which includes beautiful, natural delphinium petals. They flutter beautifully to the ground when thrown because each petal is really delicate and light.

Are the petals biodegradable? 

Yes they are. The petals are dried naturally, and are a natural product. They are usually acceptable at churches and other venues, but it is best to check. Some venues specify an area of the grounds where you are able to throw confetti.

How long do the petals last? 

The delphinium petals will last for up to a year if kept at room temperature in a dry place out of direct sunlight, so you can purchase them up to a year before your wedding if required. These delphinium petals are available all year round.

We offer natural Delphinium confetti to fill the cones. It is perfect for throwing or for decorating tables.

It comes in a beautiful colour mix, with raspberry and pale pinks, blues and purples, lilacs and ivory coloured petals.

A totally natural and biodegradable product.

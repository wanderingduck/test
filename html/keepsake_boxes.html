Gorgeous keepsake boxes which are decorated to your own design.  Strong, sturdy, white boxes in which to store those special bits and bobs that you just cannot part with after your big day. Great for your wedding cards, photographs, confetti, and of course the champagne cork from your first bottle of bubbly as Mr and Mrs! 

Great for other occasions too! Why not use it as a Honeymoon keepsake box, a Hen and Stag night box, or even a box of goodies for your Chief Bridesmaid.

Decoration – we use thick, double faced satin ribbon, a range of quality embellishments to match your theme along with Swarovski crystal detailing. Any wording is hand cut from boards to match your colour scheme. (see image to the right). We even supply a sheet of quality tissue paper in a matching colour for the inside of the box.

Simply email or telephone us with ideas for the decoration.  It is helpful to let us know the following details:

  • Name of recipient/s which you might like included
  • Any dates
  • Favourite colours / wedding colours to be used                     
  • Decorative embellishments such as butterflies, hearts, roses, gerberas, golf clubs, (we can find most things for you)
  • Message to be included
  • Venue,  Honeymoon destination or details of the Hen / Stag celebrations

Once we have all of these details and have received your payment, then we will work out some design ideas and  send you the  details for your approval. Your box will be with you within 3 weeks! If you need it more quickly contact us to see if we can help!

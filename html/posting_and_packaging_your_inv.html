It is vital that great care is taken when
packing and sending your wedding
invitations.

After the care taken to design
and create your stationery it is vital that you apply the correct postage so that your lucky guests receive your gorgeous invitations.


This page includes everything you need to know and a link to the Royal Mail website for additional information.


Additional packaging options are included below should you require them.

CLICK THE IMAGE to be taken to the Royal Mail website. This gives you all the information you need to post your invitations anywhere in the world. Use the information as a guide to pricing, but we recommend you always take your items to a post office for weighing and checking.

OTHER USEFUL ADVICE

We recommend that you always include ‘sender’ details on the back of the box or envelope. You only need to include your house number and postcode as follows: Sender: 3 SO50 7PA

On the front of the box or envelope we strongly recommend that you write a little note at the bottom which states what is enclosed as follows: ‘Wedding Invitation Enclosed / Handle With Care’

Each of our stationery designs is supplied with high quality envelopes in cream or white as standard. Designs that feature raised embellishments are supplied with boxes. You can of course upgrade any design to include coloured envelopes or boxes if you prefer. The extra costs are listed below with each option.

You may decide to upgrade the envelopes or choose boxes for all of your invitations, or just a few special invites such as those going to your bridesmaids, your parents or your special friends.

Coloured Envelope Option- 25p extra per invitation

These envelopes are wonderfully thick at 135gsm (most envelopes are 90 to100gsm) and come in a range of stunning colours to compliment your invitation designs. We can choose the envelope colour which we feel best suits your stationery or you can take your pick from the colours shown below. Just make a note of the colour when placing your order with us. A flash of vibrant colour will stand out when it lands on your guest’s doormat. It also adds to that all important first impression of your upcoming wedding.

Boxed Invitation Option- 90p extra per invitation

Our designs with raised embellishments have boxes included in the price.

For real luxury and to impress your guests your invitations can be individually tissue wrapped and boxed.

The boxes are carefully designed to meet Royal Mail’s large letter dimensions, so all you need is a large letter stamp to post them. PLEASE ENSURE YOU CHECK THE WEIGHT WITH THE POST OFFICE FIRST THOUGH. Your invitations will come to you ready wrapped and boxed. All you need to do is write your guest’s address on the box, or use a printed label. We then recommend that you use clear tape to seal the box closed.

NEW: we can now offer our invitation boxes in different colours or patterned finishes. If you are interested in this option please EMAIL US for full details. Prices available on request.

Extra thick white board envelopes– 45p each
155mm x 155mm

Really strong and thick white board envelopes. Great to add extra protection to delicate contents. Particularly useful if you have invitations that do not require boxes, but need some extra protection.

The envelopes are sized to fit our square invitations in their standard or coloured envelopes. Great if you want to send some invites abroad.

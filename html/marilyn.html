The ‘Marilyn’ Design
If you would like to order a sample or place your order please scroll to the bottom of the page!

‘Diamonds are a girls best friend’ as Marilyn said,  and whilst these are of course not real diamonds, they look utterly gorgeous. If you love your little luxuries, and you want your guests to be totally wowed by your stationery, then this is certainly the design for you.

A design that oozes quality!

A dazzling design featuring beautiful crystal embellishments with incredible sparkle. Each one is set with 16 individual brilliant cut crystal stones and measures approximately 2cm x 2cm. The embellishment is mounted onto a double layer of satin and organza ribbon as shown in the images above.

Each invitation is presented in a white postal box which is lined with      tissue. This protects the embellishment in transit and perfectly presents your invitations adding to the wow effect! (See below left).

A huge range of coordinating items are available in this design, with each featuring the embellishment or Swarovski crystal detailing as shown below right

ABOVE LEFT: Postal boxes. These protect invitations in transit, and are the correct dimensions to be sent as a Royal Mail large letter.

ABOVE RIGHT: Swarovski crystals. These measure 3mm in diameter and can be placed to accent the insert wording and on rsvp cards for example.

Your choice of wording can be printed onto the front of the card in the colour of your choice. The initials of the bride and  groom are watermarked behind this wording. For an example of how this looks see the images at the top of this page.

Base card colour and texture. We have chosen what we feel is the most suitable for each design. If you would like to change it, choose from the options below. If you have a particular card colour in mind, let us know and we will find it for you!

OPTION 1 - IVORY - For our card designs we use a stunning card which is ivory in colour. The colour is not white or cream. It suits all of our designs well and also matches well with all colour schemes. It also works well at wedding venues with white table linen. The card has subtle light reflecting particles which look beautiful when the card catches the light. It is a good weight, and all printed inserts are on matching paper.

OPTION 2 - FLAT CREAM - Smooth cream card. A classic matt card finish. A high quality heavy weight card. Inserts printed on matching paper.

OPTION 3 - FLAT WHITE - Smooth white card. A classic matt card finish. A high quality heavy weight card. Inserts on matching paper.

Ribbon colours - We use only the highest quality ribbons for our stationery.

  • Luxurious double faced satins that tie beautifully and look utterly sumptuous
  • Wonderfully light, sheer organza ribbons for a more delicate look. These ribbons have a subtle light reflecting quality.

Both ribbons are available in a huge range of gorgeous colours. If you do not see your colour, simply email us and we will see what we can do to help.

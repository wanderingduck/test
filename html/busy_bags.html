....FOR YOUR YOUNGER GUESTS

What are busy bags?

Busy bags are high quality, strong, laminated bags with colour matching rope handles. They come with a beautifully decorated and personalised tag with your little guest’s name along with a message from you and the embellishments of your choice.

Each bag is supplied filled with goodies from the selection shown below, or empty for you to fill youself.

Just let us know the following in an email and we will contact you to confirm your order:

♥ Choose the most appropriate contents

♥ Choose the embellishments to decorate the tag and bag

♥ Let us know each child’s name as you want it printed on the tag

♥ Let us know the message you would like to have printed onto the tag

High quality, matt laminated gift bags with matching rope handles. 25cm wide and 36cm high.

QUALITY BAGS

As with everything we offer, these bags are far from standard.

Our bags feature:

  • Luxury 210gsm board
  • Gorgeous smooth matt finish
  • Matching rope handles
  • Reinforced card top and base

CONTENTS – we have divided the bag contents into 4 groups to help you choose the most suitable contents:

1.Younger girls - aged 3 to 5

2. Older girls - aged 6+

3. Younger boys - aged 3 to 5

4. Older boys – aged 6+

IMPORTANT

We always do our best to keep these products in stock.

We reserve the right to substitute any of the bag contents should one of the items listed above be unavailable for any reason.

If this is the case we will always check with you first to ensure you are happy with our replacement product.

Bag decoration

Each bag is decorated with a large personalised tag measuring 10cm wide x 20cm in length. This tag is tied to the bag with a nice, thick satin ribbon in a colour to match the tag and decoration and is eyeleted for strength.

The tags are printed with the name of each child and the message of your choice, or simply the date of the wedding.  The embellishment that you choose from the selection below  is mounted onto a panel of patterned background paper. The background papers are shown below. We will choose the paper which best matches your embellishment choice.  We then add sparkles or sequins so that each bag is unique!

EMBELLISHMENT CHOICE
All Embellishments are embroidered, except for the polka dot bears which are padded satin.

They are extremely high quality and are very detailed. They vary in size. (The images below are not to scale).

Some of the embellishments are subject to a small additional charge which is also indicated under each embellishment image. 

OTHER EMBELLISHMENTS
We can also make bespoke embellishments. If your guest would not like any of the embellishments below please contact us to discuss your requirements as we can still help!

PRICES

Each fully personalised, decorated and filled busy bag is £13.90. This includes the contents which we have listed above. The contents are packed in pink tissue paper for girls and blue tissue paper for boys.

If you require additional items in your bag which you would like us to source for you please contact us and we will do all we can to help you.

Decorated busy bags with no contents are priced at £6.50 each. Perfect for you to fill yourself.

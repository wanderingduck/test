Make life easy for your guests

With extra information cards and rsvp cards designed to coordinate with the rest of your stationery your guests can be well informed. Whilst this adds to your stationery costs it is worth every penny as your guests are likely to confirm attendance quickly.

With menu and dietary requirement requests you can ensure you will not need to chase these details at a time when you could be planning your hen or stag do!

 All information is presented in a clear and stylish way. Fonts and colours can coordinate with the rest of your stationery. They can even be embellished if you wish. We can offer the following:

  • Response cards with pre-addressed envelopes
  • Dietary requirement requests
  • Menu selections
  • Hotel contact details
  • Directions to the church / venue
  • Guest list / holiday voucher details
  • Other information such as confetti restrictions, taxi numbers, favourite song requests, dress code etc.

We can also help you politely request that no children attend your wedding if you just cannot find the right words!

RSVP CARDS

These are10cm x 10cm square cards or 9.5cm x 11.5cm rectangular cards depending on the style and size of your invitations. They can be made in any of our card finishes. They come with matching envelopes with your address pre-printed should you wish. Alternatively they can be postcard style. (Other sizes of rsvp card can be supplied should you wish.)

Your rsvp cards can be simply printed in your choice of font style and colour. To add a bit of sparkle a gorgeous little Swarovski crystal can be added.

Extra Information Cards

Any information that your guests may need can be presented as a simple card.

For a beautifully coordinated look we will match the card type, font style and colour to your other stationery items. The size of the information card is your choice, but it looks great if it is the same size as your invite so that it sits neatly in the box or envelope.

You must send us your information as a ‘word’ document. We will organise the text so that it fits neatly onto one or two sides of the card and will send you an email proof for your approval. If you would like a printed map you are responsible for obtaining permission to use it.

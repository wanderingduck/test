YOUR OPTIONS

  • All tags are in a colour to match your favour box
    colour. They are cut from a high quality white, ivory or pale cream board.
  • Eyelets come in silver or gold.
  • The ribbon that attaches the tag is in a colour to match your favour box as this looks best in our

Every tag includes the following:
  • Tags cut from either white, ivory or cream board.

  • 7mm double faced satin ribbon to match card colour.
  • Printed name and / or message in your choice of font style and colour.
  • Tags can either be attached to our favour boxes or supplied pre-strung and ready for you to attach yourself to your own items
